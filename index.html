Our Story

A Lakeside Sanctuary Like No Other

Founded in 2010, Nyanza Pearl Lodge was born from a vision to share the breathtaking beauty of Lake Victoria with the world. Situated just minutes from Kisumu city centre, our lodge blends contemporary comfort with authentic Luo culture.

Every corner of our property has been designed to frame the magnificent lake — from sunrise breakfasts on the waterfront terrace to sundowner cocktails watching fishermen return with their catch.

Activities

Curated Experiences

🚣
Lake Victoria Sunset Cruise

Glide across Africa's largest lake as the sun paints the water gold. Our traditional wooden dhow carries you through the evening light while our guide shares the legends of the Luo people.

From KES 3,500 / person
🦅
Impala Sanctuary Bird Walk

Kisumu Impala Sanctuary is home to over 350 bird species. Our expert ornithologist guides you at dawn through papyrus wetlands and acacia woodland for an unforgettable experience.

From KES 2,000 / person
🎣
Traditional Fishing Excursion

Join local fishermen at sunrise for an authentic experience on the lake. Learn centuries-old fishing techniques and bring your catch back for our chef to prepare for your dinner.

From KES 4,500 / person

Getting Here

Located just 8 minutes from Kisumu International Airport and 5 minutes from Kisumu CBD, Nyanza Pearl Lodge is perfectly positioned for both leisure and business travellers.

  • ✈️
    By Air 8 min from Kisumu International Airport — complimentary airport transfers available
  • 🚗
    By Road 5 min from Kisumu CBD via Oginga Odinga Street — ample secure parking on site
  • 🚤
    By Boat Direct boat transfers available from Mbita, Homa Bay, and Kendu Bay
